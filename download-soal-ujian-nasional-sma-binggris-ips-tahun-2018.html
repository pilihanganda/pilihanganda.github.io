Permutasi P = n!/k!

• B.Inggris → 8 huruf

unsur ganda : (i) = 2 , (g) = 2

P = n!/k!

P = 8!/2! . 2!

P = (8.7.6.5.4.3.2) / (2. 1 . 2 . 1)
