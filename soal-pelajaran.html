34. soal mata pelajaran matematika​

y=a(x-x1)(x-x2)

Dengan:

(-6,0)----->x1=-6

(-2,0)----->x2=-2

y=a(x-(-6))(x-(-2))

y=a(x+6)(x+2)

y=a(x²+8x+12)

y=ax²+8ax+12a

y=x²+8x+12
