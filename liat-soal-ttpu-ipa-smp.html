V = I. R₁ = 1. 12 = 12 v

Setiap lampu mempunyai tegangan sebesar V = 12 v.

Arus di lampu 2

I₂ = V/R₂ = 12/6 = 2 A

Arus di lampu 3

I₃ = V/R₃ = 12/3 = 4 A

Daya pada setiap lampu

P₁ = I₁². R₁ = 1². 12 = 12 watt

P₂ = I₂². R₂ = 2². 6 = 24 watt

P₃ = I₃². R₃ = 4². 3 = 48 watt
