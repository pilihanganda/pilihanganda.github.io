Maka :

La = 2 × 1/2 × Jumlah Sisi Sejajar × Tinggi
La = 2 × 1/2 × (10+16) × 8
La = 2 × 1/2 × 26 × 8
La = 26 × 8 = 208 cm²

<=> 44 × 20 = 880 cm²
