Anda akan pergi ke suatu tempat, dan belum tahu transportasi apa yang dapat digunakan untuk dapat pergi ke tempat tersebut. Anda bertanya kepada seseorang perihal transportasi apa yang bisa digunakan, dimana anda harus berhenti, berapa lama waktu yang dibutuhkan untuk mencapai tempat tujuan anda, dan berapa berapa biaya yang dikeluarkan. Perhatikan​


Jawaban:

Dialog:

A: Excuse me, can you help me? I'm planning to go to a place, but I'm not sure which transportation I can use to get there.

B: Sure, I'd be happy to help. Where are you planning to go?

A: I want to go to the city center. What transportation options are available?

B: Well, you can take a bus or a taxi. The bus is a more affordable option, while the taxi is more convenient but slightly more expensive.

A: I see. Where should I catch the bus?

B: You can catch the bus at the bus stop just around the corner. It's a short walk from here.

A: How long does it take to reach the city center by bus?

B: It usually takes around 30 minutes, depending on the traffic.

A: And what about the taxi? How long does it take?

B: With a taxi, it should take around 15-20 minutes, again depending on the traffic.

A: I see. And how much does the bus fare cost?

B: The bus fare is usually around $2.

A: And what about the taxi fare?

B: The taxi fare is approximately $10.

A: Thank you so much for your help. I think I'll take the bus since it's more affordable.

B: You're welcome! Have a safe trip!

A: Thank you, I will. Goodbye!

B: Goodbye!
